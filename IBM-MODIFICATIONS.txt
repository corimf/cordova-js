- Cordova RTC work item number: 59037

- Markmail / Google Group / StackOverflow reference: 
- Product PMR: 41288,057,649

- Description: After fix the window.blackberry.invoke(), part of the blackberry plugins, 
  an optimization to the added code was required to 3.4.0esr, and it's required by 3.6.1esr as well.
  window.webworks.exec is non-required, what's required is 'webworksready' event.
  As part of the optimization, support for Webworks 2.x was added in the code.
  Now projects can handle WW 1.x and in case of upgrade, it can support WW 2.x as well.
